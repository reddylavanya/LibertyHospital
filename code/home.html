Our Mission

The Liberty Hospital Foundation was established in 1984 with the mission to improve the quality of health, wellness, and care in the Liberty Hospital community

Your gifts help us assist under-insured and under-served patients, support community health education and programs, provide valuable resources to hospital employees and patients, and offer pathways to education for future and current nurses in our area.
